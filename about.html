About us

Who we are & our story

We are on mission to

turn web3 illeterates to experts

Cseers is a multitudinous web3 organisation that travels back and forth through various aspects of crypto and blockchain technology, for the primary purpose of liberating members financially. Open to all, free for all.

Only 3.5% of the young population in Africa are well-acquainted with the web3, NFT & blockchain technology. So our goal here is to educate more young people and nuture them well enough for them to earn passive income via NFTS, Defi, and crypto as a whole.

Furthermore, CSeersDAO has a strictly no-fee web3 policy where interested people have a chance to gain knowledge on web3 & crypto for free.

Our story

It all started with closed group of around 10 to 15 people and where we dropped some Crypto calls (mostly coins). A number of users reached out to one of us asking if they had any group(s) where they could get a better understanding of our calls and testimonies (this led to the creation of the mini groups) and how we could come together to see if it was possible to create legitimate, stress-free wealth using the web3.

Within three to six months, Cseers became a force to be reckoned with as a result of the wealth generated from knowledge shared among individuals.

It wasn't difficult for results to spring forth as growth is inevitable for success, which led to the creation of more mini-groups where knowledge can be taught, shared, and leveraged.

Why "CSEERS"

Getting a name was a bit technical but in the long run we coined out a name.

I wanted a name that sounded a bit more prophetic (since we believe we have all it takes to nurture and guide users in wealth creation) - CRYPTO PROPHET was what I thought of, but had to look deeper till the name CRYPTO SEER appeared.

- DTWO. Founder, CSeersDAO

Over the years we thought that since we didn't just focus on crypto, but also other spheres of wealth generation(NFT's,Web3,and Blockchain technology related ideas) we narrowed the name to CSEER. This was the beginning of expansion and it became a full-fledged Dao, where users could access tools that would generate wealth over time.

We're not quite there yet, but we feel we are just a few blocks away from making Cseer's a benchmark for both web3 and the space as a whole.
