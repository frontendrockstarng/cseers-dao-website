About us

Who we are & our story

We are on mission to

turn web3 illeterates to experts

Cseers is a multitudinous web3 organisation that travels back and forth through various aspects of crypto and blockchain technology, for the primary purpose of liberating members financially.

Only 3.5% of the young population in Africa are well-acquainted with web3. So, our goal is to educate more young people and nuture them well enough so they could earn passive income via nfts, DeFi, crypto, and web3 as a whole.

Furthermore, CSeersDAO has a strictly no-fee web3 policy where interested people have a chance to gain knowledge on web3 for free. Open to all, free for all.

Our story

It all started with a closed group of 10 to 15 people where airdrops and crypto signals were dropped. A number of users reached out to the founder "Dtwo" with a request to join the group having heard good testimony from the few existing members. The founder "Dtwo" saw it as an opportunity to bring people together with the common goal of creating legitimate, stress-free wealth opportunity for people via web3.

Within three to six months, CseersDAO became a force to be reckoned with as a result of the wealth generated from knowledge and classic information shared among community members.

Since creation, lots of young talents have been bred and have been set to financial breakthroughs through the accurate and timely information, materials and motivations made easily and readily available for her members.

Why "CSEERS"

Getting the name "CseersDAO" was a bit technical as this is not just a name but a brand.

I wanted to give it a name that sound prophetic (since I know we have all it takes to onboard, nurture and guide a successful influx of new users in web3) - CRYPTO PROPHET was the first thought that came to mind, but I had to think deeper till the name Crypto SEER came through.

- DTWO. Founder, CSeersDAO

Over the years, we realised our community ain't focused only on crypto but also other areas in web3 (including NFTs, airdrops and DeFi ), we abridged the name to CSEERS. This was the beginning of expansion and it became a full-fledged DAO, where users are further granted easy access to more opportunities for quality information and wealth generation.

We've been building and we will continue building until we make CseersDAO a benchmark for web3 and the entire space as a whole.
